Attention to detail

Attention to detail

A client purchasing a sizeable property required advice on the impact of the Corporate Interest Restriction regulations on their financing arrangement. I produced a model in Microsoft Excel that illustrated the impacts of a variety of levels of debt over the course of their 6-year timeline. This provided meaningful insight into the tax-loss of the proposed structure, so that it could be amended accordingly.
