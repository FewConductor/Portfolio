About me

I am a friendly and enthusiastic junior developer with a love for learning more. I enjoy both rigorous detail and meeting new people, and am excited to be setting out on my software journey.

After finishing my Classics degree at Cambridge University, I spent 6 years at a London-based accountancy firm. During this time I qualified as both a chartered accountant and a chartered tax adviser, achieving the highest mark in the UK in each of my advanced technical tax exams.

In 2024, I left the firm to travel in East Asia for a few months, after which I joined a 12-week Software Development bootcamp. This has taught me a number of front- and back-end languages, setting me up well for my transition into a junior full-stack developer role.

Outside work I enjoy field hockey, which I have been playing for 17 years, various forms of dance - currently Scottish reeling - and cooking. I like to keep busy and set myself challenges, most recently completing a marathon in under 4 hours.
